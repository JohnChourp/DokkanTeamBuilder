Cookies Delete Policy
Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide you with relevant information. However, we understand that you may wish to delete cookies stored on your device for privacy reasons. Here's how to delete cookies on the most popular browsers:
  • Google Chrome
    Click on the three dots icon at the top right corner of the browser, select "More tools" > "Clear browsing data." Select the time range and tick the box next to "Cookies and other site data." Click "Clear data.". More info at:
    Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
    Click on the three lines icon at the top right corner of the browser, select "Options" > "Privacy & Security." Scroll down to the "Cookies and Site Data" section and click "Clear Data." Tick the box next to "Cookies and Site Data" and click "Clear." More info at:
    Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
    Click on the "Safari" tab at the top left corner of the browser and select "Preferences." Click on the "Privacy" tab and then click "Manage Website Data." Select the website whose cookies you want to delete and click "Remove." More info at:
    Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
    Click on the three dots icon at the top right corner of the browser and select "Settings" > "Privacy, search, and services." Click on "Choose what to clear" under "Clear browsing data" and select "Cookies and other site data." Click "Clear now." More info at:
    Microsoft Edge
Please note that deleting cookies may affect your browsing experience and you may need to log in again on some websites.
